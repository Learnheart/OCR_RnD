3. (Singapore) Let $f$ be a polynomial of degree $n$, such that $f(k) = \frac{1}{k+1}$ for every integer $0 \leq k \leq n$. Compute $f(n+1)$.

4. (USA) Let $f$ be a real polynomial of degree $n$, such that $f(k) = \binom{n}{k}^{-1}$ for $0 \leq k \leq n$. Compute all possible values of $f(n+1)$.

5. (IMO—shortlist) A polynomial $f$ of degree 996, is such that $f(k) = F_k$ for $992 \leq k \leq 1982$, where $F_k$ stands for the $k$-th Fibonacci number. Prove that $f(1983) = F_{1983} - 1$.

6. (IMO—shortlist) Let $f$ be a real polynomial such that

(a) $f(1) > f(0) > 0$.

(b) $f(2) > 2f(1) - f(0)$.

(c) $f(3) > 3f(2) - 3f(1) + f(0)$.

(d) $f(n+4) > 4f(n+3) - 6f(n+2) + 4f(n+1) - f(n)$ for every $n \in \mathbb{N}$.

Prove that $f(n) > 0$ for every $n \in \mathbb{N}$.
