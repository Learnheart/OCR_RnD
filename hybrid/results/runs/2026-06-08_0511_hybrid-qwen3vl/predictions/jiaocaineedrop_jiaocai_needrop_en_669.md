二、阅读理解（共15小题，每小题2分，满分30分）
阅读下列短文，从每题的四个选项（A、B、C和D）中，选出最佳选项，并在答题卡上将该选项涂黑。

Mary Cassatt, considered one of America’s most famous female artists.
She was born in Pennsylvania in 1844. When she was a child, Mary and her family spent five years travelling and living in Europe. Mary saw the great art museums, and wanted to become an artist.
When she was seventeen, she said that she wanted to go to Paris to study art. At that time, people thought that only men could become artists. It was not considered proper work for a woman. Her father refused to let her study art. Finally she said she could not go to Paris and she went to a school near her home.
She attended the Pennsylvania Academy of Fine Arts.
At her age of 18, in 1862, she said that she had to go abroad to learn more about painting. Her father agreed that he would let her go. As she sailed for Paris, she had to learn to paint in Europe. She spent a lot of time in museums. She worked hard to improve her skills. She studied the famous paintings of the old masters and the copied and sketched (临摹) works for hours.
She called herself impressionist. (印象派) Like them, Mary Cassatt tried to show real people and the great artist that she was.
