# Three out of four executives think that in the next decade, industrywide investments in virtual health will be significantly higher than today

The virtual health market in the United States is projected to grow more than 23 percent annually over the next five years, reaching more than $128 billion by 2025, according to a recent estimate.1

Our surveyed executives predict industrywide investments in virtual health in the next decade will be significantly higher than today—25 percent anticipate that investments in virtual health will increase by 25 percent or more (Figure 3).
