Bureau of Land Management (BLM) (RIN 1034-AD87)
Notice of Likely Action: Modified Competitive Sale of 30 Acre Lot of Public Land in Lincoln County and White Pine County, Nevada
AGENCY: Bureau of Land Management.
ACTION: Notice of likely action.
SUMMARY: The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) proposes to offer seven (7) parcels of public land totaling 371.3 acres in Lincoln County and White Pine County to modified competitive sale of the public land pursuant to the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (FLPMA) and the White Pine County Conservation, Recreation, and Development Act of 2004 (WPCDCA). The sale will be processed in accordance with applicable land use planning and environmental laws and regulations. The sale will be conducted on or before September 12, 2024, at 8:00 a.m., Pacific time, in the BLM’s office located at https://www.bureauoflandmanagement.gov/ regarding the sale until August 21, 2024. BLM will publish notices for the sale in the Lincoln County and White Pine County newspapers. Prior to the sale, a solicitation will be published in the following locations: https://www.bureauoflandmanagement.gov/ specific to each parcel and such listing will be posted on the BLM’s website at https://www.bureauoflandmanagement.gov/ for each parcel that will be available for sale. The BLM will not accept any bids for the land until the official notice is published in the Federal Register. The BLM will not accept any bids for the land until the official notice is published in the Federal Register.
